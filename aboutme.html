I am a 16 year old sophmore at Seattle Academy. I partisipate in Robotics club and Lacrosse in the spring time. I ski in the winter, bike when I can and love to do anything outdoors.

I love to take pictures. I have abother website where I have collected some of my different photographs. Click on the image of the site bellow to check it out.

Every summer for the past 8 years I have attended a month long summer camp in Colorado Called Sanborn Western Camps. This place has shaped who I am today. Without the experiences I have had at Sanborn and the things I have learned there I would be a very different person. I have learned leadeship skills, outdoor ethics, outdoor survival skils, outdoor navigational skills, how to be prepared, and how to have safe fun in the backcountry. I have many friend from around the country that I see only there year after year.
