Sculpture


Plaster


Cast Hand

Grenade

This hand holding a grenade was done by casting my had, while holding that plastic grenade, in some molding gel. After I cast my hand, i poured in liquid plaster. Once the plaster has cured, I cut out the molding gel. I was left with the hand and the grenade, which is stuck in the grip without any glue.

Foot

Carved Foot

To make this plaster foot, I formed out of cardboard, and then poured large block of plaster. Once it was partially cured I removed the cardboard mold and began carving. I used chisels, a cheese grater like thing, sandpaper, and many other tools to create the curvature of the foot.

Ceramic


Bowl 1

Wavy Bowl

This piece was made on a wheel before I made it wavy around the edges. I then glazed the inside with a speckled color while I used a pewter/metallic paint on the outside.

Bowl 2

Gray Stripe

This bowl was made by rolling a large piece of clay flat and then molding it to another large bowl. After it had a little time to harden, I used a knife to cut the tops of the edges in a wavy fashion. For glazing, I painted it all green and then used tape to mark of a stripe down the middle. I used a gray glaze for the stripe which goes around the entire piece, top to bottom.

Bowl 3

Small Speckle

For this small bowl, I used a pottery wheel. I created the small bowl and then glazed it with a bright speckled glaze. It can be used to store small jewelry or other small things.
