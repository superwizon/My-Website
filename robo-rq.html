My Contributions

This year on 2856 I have been a designer, builder, and driver. I have played a large role in the design of our robot. After we designed it, I have worked alongside my fellow members to create the robot that we have today. I am also the robot's operator.

2856's Accomplishments

We competed in our league matches, inter-league matches (where we won the inspire award), and then qualified for State (where we won the think award and second place inspire award). At State we qualified for Super Regionals whick will be March 24-26. We are currently working to prepare our robot and ouselves for the Super Regional competition. If we do well there, we have the possiblity of qualifying for Worlds in St. Louis.
