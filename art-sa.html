Sketch 3

Construction Trailer Closed

This is the closed, left, isometric view of the construction trailer I am designing for my dad's company, Hometown Builders. The idea whith this trailer is that it would be closed up and put on a trailer to transport it between job sites. While at the site, it would be unloaded and parked for th duration of the project. Inside it would be tools, desk space, and space for a generator and air compressor.

Pastel

Stripes and Forms

The People in this piece, started as pictures of me standing in interesting ways. I then drew those interesting positions as silhouette. On a large piece of paper, I used a ruler, and drew all of the diagonal lines. I used a color pastel to fill in the penciled lines. Doing this added depth. Once that was complete, I pasted the silhouetted people on the striped background.

Paint

Colorado Man

The background was painted of the Colorado flag with some added mountains and trees. A face was then drawn and pasted on top of the background.
